Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of psychosocial rehabilitation services, including cognitive skills training, social skills development, independent living skills training, and emotional support. Each service is tailored to meet your individual needs and goals.

How long does a typical program last?

Program duration varies based on individual needs and goals. Typically, programs range from 3 to 12 months, with regular assessments to track progress and adjust treatment plans accordingly.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. We also offer flexible payment options and can work with you to determine the best financial arrangement for your situation.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy! Simply schedule a free initial consultation through our website or call us directly. During this consultation, we'll discuss your needs, answer any questions, and develop a personalized treatment plan.
